About Us

Birla Institute of Technology, Mesra, established in 1954, is among India's top 50 institutions. We pioneered India's first Aerospace Engineering Department and continue to excel in education and research.

With a legacy of seven decades, BIT Mesra is renowned for world-class faculty, cutting-edge facilities, and a commitment to producing impactful graduates. We offer a wide range of programs and foster holistic development in our students.
